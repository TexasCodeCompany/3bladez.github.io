## Security & Risk Mitigation

We take the security and trustworthiness of our drone systems seriously, especially when operating in life-critical missions. Below are **known security risks** common to drone operations that we actively identify and mitigate:

- **Insecure Communication** — Risk of interception or tampering with video feeds and control commands.  
  **Mitigation**: End-to-end encryption and secure protocols (TLS).

- **Weak Authentication & Authorization** — Risk of unauthorized access to drone controls or data.  
  **Mitigation**: Multi-factor authentication and strict role-based access controls.

- **Insecure Firmware / Software** — Vulnerabilities that could allow exploitation of the drone system.  
  **Mitigation**: Regular signed updates, secure development practices, and continuous vulnerability scanning.

- **Inadequate Personal Data Protection** — Risk related to collected imagery and location data.  
  **Mitigation**: Privacy-by-design principles, data encryption, and full compliance with applicable regulations.

- **Lack of Secure Update Mechanisms** — Risk of compromised updates introducing malware.  
  **Mitigation**: Cryptographically signed updates with integrity verification.

- **Insecure Third-Party Components** — Use of vulnerable libraries or modules.  
  **Mitigation**: Rigorous vetting, timely patching, and ongoing monitoring of dependencies.

- **Insufficient Network Security** — Weaknesses in Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or other wireless interfaces.  
  **Mitigation**: Strong encryption, minimal attack surface, and disabling unnecessary services.

- **Physical Security Weaknesses** — Risk of physical tampering with the drone.  
  **Mitigation**: Tamper detection mechanisms and secure hardware design.

- **Insecure Data Storage** — Risk of sensitive data being exposed if the drone is compromised.  
  **Mitigation**: Encrypted on-device storage and remote wipe capabilities.

- **Lack of Logging & Monitoring** — Difficulty detecting breaches without proper records.  
  **Mitigation**: Comprehensive logging, real-time monitoring, and automated alerts for suspicious activity.
